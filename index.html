About Us

The Great Blue Heron

According to the North American Native tradition, the Blue Heron brings messages of self-determination and self-reliance. They represent an ability to progress and evolve.

The stunning silhouette of the heron is often used as a symbol of the natural beauty of wetland habitats. They curls its neck into a tight "S" shape and its whings are rounded with a broad "W" shape.

The initial "S" and "W" represent its two founder's names, Suhandono and Wanda.
